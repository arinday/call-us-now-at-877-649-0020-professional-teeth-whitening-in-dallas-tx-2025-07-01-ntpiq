Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the whitening treatment take?

Our professional whitening treatments typically take 60-90 minutes. You'll see results immediately, with full results developing over the next 24-48 hours. We also offer express treatments for those with time constraints.

Is professional teeth whitening safe?

Yes, our treatments are completely safe and FDA-approved. We use professional-grade products and protective measures to ensure your comfort and safety. Our experienced technicians carefully evaluate your oral health before proceeding with any treatment.

How long do the results last?

Results typically last 6-12 months with proper care. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and maintenance recommendations to help extend the longevity of your results. Touch-up treatments are available as needed.

What about sensitive teeth?

We offer special treatments for sensitive teeth using gentle formulations and desensitizing agents. Our process includes pre-treatment evaluation and customized approaches to minimize any discomfort.

What's included in the price?

Our comprehensive service includes consultation, professional whitening treatment, aftercare kit, and follow-up care. We also offer a satisfaction guarantee and free touch-ups within the first month if needed.
